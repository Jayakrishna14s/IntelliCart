The Core AI Modules

IntelliCart is powered by a suite of interconnected AI models. Each module is designed to solve a specific challenge, working together to create a seamless and optimized shopping experience.

1. Predictive Price Forecasting

Analyzes historical price data using LSTM/Prophet models to recommend the optimal time to purchase, providing clear "Wait vs. Buy" advice.

2. Dynamic User Profiling

Classifies user intent in real-time based on browsing behavior to personalize recommendations for their specific needs.

3. Cart Optimization & Bundling

An RL agent fine-tunes the cart, identifying cost-effective bundles and applying the best coupons to maximize savings.

Project Development Roadmap

Our development is structured in four distinct phases, ensuring a methodical progression from foundational setup to full-scale deployment and analysis.

Phase 1: Foundation

Setup core backend, database, and a basic UI. Establish data ingestion pipelines.

Phase 2: Core AI

Develop and train price forecasting and user profiling models. Integrate with backend API.

Phase 3: Optimization

Implement cart optimization, bundling, and delivery prediction. Enhance frontend to display recommendations.

Phase 4: Deployment

Containerize with Docker, integrate caching, and conduct supply chain intelligence study.
